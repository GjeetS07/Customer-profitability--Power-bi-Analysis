# Customer Profitability Analysis (Power BI Project)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard focused on analyzing customer profitability. The objective is to identify high-value customers, understand revenue and cost distribution, and support strategic decision making for business growth.

## Tools and Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Visualization
* Power Query (Data Transformation)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

## Dashboard Features

* Customer-wise revenue and profit analysis
* Identification of high-value and low-value customer segments
* Profitability distribution across different categories
* Trend analysis of revenue and profit
* Interactive filters for customer segmentation

## Key Insights

* A small percentage of customers contribute to a large share of total profit
* Certain customer segments generate high revenue but low profit due to higher costs
* Profitability varies significantly across customer groups
* Identified underperforming segments that require optimization
